Growth Strategy
Organic & Paid Growth

A dual-track growth strategy — organic reach builds authenticity and loyalty, while paid boosting amplifies the best-performing content to qualified new audiences.

Organic Growth

Reach Velocity

Consistent posting cadence, strategic timing, and Reel-first content to leverage Instagram's algorithmic push for video content — increasing weekly organic reach progressively.

Qualified Follower Base

Content designed to attract fitness enthusiasts, gym-goers, and performance-minded individuals — not just vanity followers. Quality over quantity, every time.

Engagement Rate Improvement

Active community management — responding to every comment and DM within 4 hours — signals trust and boosts algorithmic visibility simultaneously.

Paid Growth (from Month 2)

Amplified Best Performers

Budget is directed behind content that already demonstrates organic traction — ensuring every rupee spent reaches the highest probability of a real, interested user.

Audience Targeting

Custom audiences built from website visitors, video viewers, and profile engagers. Lookalike audiences expand reach to high-intent users mirroring existing fans.

Retargeting Funnel

Month 3 ads retarget users who engaged with Month 2 content — moving warm leads from awareness into consideration and ultimately, purchase intent.

Projected Growth Direction

1
Month 1
Organic foundation. Grid built. Brand presence established. Gradual follower growth begins.
2
Month 2
Boosting activates. Reach expands significantly. Engagement rate improves. Follower acquisition accelerates.
3
Month 3
Compounding effect. Warm audiences convert. Profile becomes a trusted destination for performance-minded buyers.

Market Intelligence
Competitor Analysis

Understanding the landscape helps Athlisis position itself distinctly — identifying gaps others have left and owning them with precision.

BlissClub
@myblissclub · Women's Activewear
488K Instagram Followers

Community-first model

BlissClub built its brand on community before products — WhatsApp groups, Instagram polls, and customer input in product design. The result: a deeply loyal, engaged following that advocates organically.

UGC as primary content

A significant portion of their feed features real customers in their gear — diverse body types, authentic settings. This builds trust and massively reduces content production costs.

1,990+ posts, consistent cadence

Relentless posting consistency over four years has compounded into near 500K followers. Their engagement is driven by product polls, movement challenges, and community spotlights.

HRX by Hrithik Roshan
@hrxbrand · Celebrity-led Activewear
1.2M+ Instagram Followers

Celebrity-driven authority

HRX leverages Hrithik Roshan's personal fitness identity to build brand credibility. Every product launch is amplified through his personal channel, giving them extraordinary organic reach.

High-production content

Cinematic reels, professional photography, and editorial-quality campaign visuals signal premium positioning. Their content stops the scroll with visual intensity and aspirational energy.

Omnichannel distribution

Available across Myntra, Flipkart, and own D2C platforms — social media acts as awareness and brand-building, while marketplace listings handle volume sales.

The Athlisis Opportunity

Where Competitors Win

Community depth (BlissClub), celebrity reach (HRX), and mass distribution. These are advantages built over years and significant capital investment.

Where Athlisis Can Own

The raw, authentic performance aesthetic — men's physique and athletic training — is under-served in India. Premium quality at an accessible price point is a powerful differentiator that can be owned through consistent, high-quality content storytelling.

Paid Strategy · Month 2 Onwards
Ad Budget Allocation

A disciplined ₹10,000 monthly ad budget, split between top-of-funnel awareness and mid-funnel engagement — structured to maximise return at every stage.

₹10,000 / month
ad budget
Top of Funnel — Reach & Awareness ₹6,000
Middle of Funnel — Engagement ₹3,000
Testing & Optimisation Reserve ₹1,000
Month 3 shift: As warm audiences build, budget allocation shifts — reducing TOF spend and increasing retargeting (bottom-of-funnel) to drive conversion from engaged users.
Top of Funnel · ₹6,000

Reach & Brand Awareness

Boost the highest-performing Reels and static posts to cold audiences. Interest targeting: fitness, gym, sportswear, bodybuilding. Goal: maximise impressions and video views among new, relevant users in India. Estimated reach: 80,000–1,20,000 unique users/month.

Middle of Funnel · ₹3,000

Engagement & Profile Visits

Retarget users who watched 50%+ of boosted videos or visited the profile. Objective: engagement and website click campaigns. Content: product carousels, testimonial posts, and drop teasers. Goal: move warm leads closer to purchase intent.

Platforms

All boosting runs through Meta Business Suite, placing ads simultaneously on Instagram Feed, Instagram Reels, Instagram Stories, and Facebook Feed — maximising reach per rupee spent without requiring separate campaign builds.
